---Vanilla Cake---

Ingredients

200g Self-Rising Flour
200g Caster Sugar
200g Butter
3 Eggs
1 Tablespoons Milk
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1 Teaspoon Baking Powder

Extra Ingredients

Double Cream
340g Icing Sugar
150g Butter
Lindor
Hersheys
Small bottle of Alcohol (optional)
Edible Pearls
Sprinkles

Also:

If you want to decorate your cake like this or somthing similar the instructions are down below.
It is a 2 tier, 8 inch cake so you will need to double the batches and make 2 cakes.

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease two 10-inch cake pans, line with parchment paper. Parchment paper helps the cakes seamlessly release from the pans.
2. Using an electric whisk or stand mixer beat the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
3. Separate the egg yolks from the whites. Add in the egg yolk with vanilla and set the egg whites aside.
4. Make sure to scrape down the sides of the bowl.
5. Fold in the flour and baking powder unitl fully combined then add the milk.
6. Whisk the egg whites until it has fluffy peaks then mix that into the cake batter.
7. Bake in the centre of the oven for 25-30 mins until a skewer inserted into the middle of each cake comes out clean.

Further Instructions

1. Beat together the butter and icing sugar on a medium/high setting on the mixer.
2.Add the vanilla and double cream after it is combined and mix again.
3. Coat the whole cake in buttercream making sure it is coated evenly.
4. In a bowl heat the double cream for 45 seconds and mix with white chocolate untip melted. Add any colour you want and pourour this ontop of the cake making drips as you go.
5. In a piping bag put in some buttercream and pipe whatever design you want on the top and botttom.
6. Place any amount of lindor chocolates and hersheys you want on top evenly.
7. Place however amount of oreos you want on top evenly.
8. Rollout a small chunky of fondant and cut it into a banner shape.
9. Print who's ever name the cake is for on the fondant and stick to the cake.

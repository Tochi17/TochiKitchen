---Sponge Cake---

Ingredients

225g Self-Raising Flour
225g Caster Sugar
225g Butter
4 Eggs
2 Tablespoons Milk
1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
120g Raspberry / Strawberry Jam
150ml Double Cream

Extra Ingredients

Double Cream
340g Icing Sugar
150g Butter
Oreos
Sprinkles



Also:

If you want to decorate your cake like this or somthing similar the instructions are down below.
It is a 2 tier, 8 inch cake so you will need to double the batches and make 2 cakes.

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Grease two 10-inch cake pans, line with parchment paper. Parchment paper helps the cakes seamlessly release from the pans.
2. Using an electric whisk or stand mixer beat the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
3. Crack the eggs in one at a time and whisk well, scraping down the sides of the bowl after each addition.
4. Add the lemon zest, vanilla, flour, milk and a pinch of salt.
5. Whisk until just combined then divide the mixture between the two tins.
6. Bake in the centre of the oven for 25-30 mins until a skewer inserted into the middle of each cake comes out clean.
7. After 10 mins remove the cakes from their tins and leave to cool completely on a wire rack.
8. Fill how you like. My personal favourite is a good spread of raspberry jam and a dollop of fresh whipped cream.

Instructions

1. Beat together the butter and icing sugar on a medium/high setting on the mixer.
2.Add the vanilla and double cream after it is combined and mix again.
3. Add your desired colour and coat the whole cake in buttercream making sure it is coated evenly.
4. In a bowl heat the double cream for 45 seconds and mix with white chocolate untip melted.
5. Add any colour you want and pour this ontop of the cake making drips as you go.
6. In a piping bag put in some buttercream and pipe whatever design you want on the top and botttom.
7. Place however amount of oreos you want on top evenly.
8. Sprinlke what ever edible decoration you want ontop and around the cake. As you can see I used sprinkles.

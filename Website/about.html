About Me

Hi guys, welcome to my page! My name is Tochi. I am a college student studying Creative Digital Media. I am from Dublin, and as a young girl I have always been intrested in baking from watching baking shows to youtube video to watching my mom making pastries and treats. Well, I think I started baking for people outside around 2013 it was just an easy way to share my baking recipes with friends and family. I was making little things like sausage rolls, muffins, biscuits / cookies, etc.

My Journey

I always liked baking from a young age but I guess it had gotten strong when I started 1st year in secondary school, I had taken Home Economics. This is where I learned at picked up on most of my baking skills.Home Economics opened my love for baking even though I absolutely hated my teacher(whoops). I loved the joy of preparing, baking and then taste what I had made.

But I started making cakes for family and close friends first lets say 5 years ago, it was just for learning purposes. But as I grew up people were noticing my cakes and were wanting to buy of me , and I ending up getting small contracts here and there. Now my talent and design has improved and more people are coming for my beautiful cakes and I even finally set up an Instagram page which is linked down below.
